What we’re working on?

Hacking with your teammates is always better than going solo, especially in offline hackathons. Our website tries to make it as easy as it can be to find teammates for your next hackathon. While many hackathon specific websites like Devfolio and Devpost exist, it isn’t easy to find and make teams there. With our website, users can see all the hackathons listed and then move to the chat room to interact with other hackers who will be attending that particular hackathon. They can then choose to make teams or just get to know other hackers, it’s upto them. Our UI is simple and minimal so that we can avoid all the clutter and help you get what you want - your hackathon teammates.
